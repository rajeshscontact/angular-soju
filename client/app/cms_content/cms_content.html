Would you like to use a CMS for your project?

Please change the feature toggle to true in featureToggle service and configure your CMS. We would recommend to use Contentful as a CMS.
You can proceed to the Contentful Website and follow their instructions on how to create your space, add content and assets as well as how to retrieve and include them into your application.
Depending on the needs and complexity of your application you might want to utilize multiple tools and methods combined with a chosen CMS like content cache, strong processing engine, etc.
We have a folder structure set up for you for a simple retrieval of the content. Please see the image below. It ilustrates how the result may look in a simple use case that can be easily achieved with the proposed structure. The title, the author, the date, the text and the image are all separate entries in the CMS.
